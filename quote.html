There are many stories from George Bernard Shaw - perhaps too many, but the best remains to be told. An eyewitness told me about it. It was at the premiere of "Back To Methuselah". The theater rages with enthusiasm and calls for the author. But at the moment when Shaw finally appears in front of the curtain, a loud whistle from the gallery mixes with the applause. Bernard Shaw waves warmly to the gallery and calls out: "My friend! I am completely of the same opinion with you! - But what can we both do against such a majority!"

George Bernard Shaw

Playwriter 1856 - 1950
