Alsdann schwang er sich in die wunderschöne Limousine, steckte den Schlüssel ins Schloss, hielt nochmals kurz hinter der Gartenhecke an, um die Tragetasche mit den anderen alten Zwergen einzusammeln und atmete tief durch. Es war der schönste Atemzug seit langem. Seine Hand fuhr in die Hosentasche, wo er die Diamanten fühlte, dann strich er über die Limousine. Noch nie hatte das Leben es so gut mit ihm gemeint. Freudig rief er aus „Leben ich komme!“ Dann gab er Gas und ward in dieser Gegend nie wieder gesehen.

With one jump he ran into the car garage and was back in no time with a division. He had 13 minutes until dinner at 7 p.m. 13 minutes that should change his life. With the help of the saving he pushed aside one of the turf strips, then hastily dug a hole in which he buried the larger human remains. Then he carefully covered everything with the turf again, distributed the new garden gnomes where their predecessors had stood and hurried back to the car garage. Now it was up to him. With an excited hand he wrote the following lines:


My dear Carol - you are the great love of my life!
How did I enjoy it with you this afternoon, dearest one, on the balcony! Unfortunately, the idiot in the garden annoyed me so much that I went down to him before dinner to give him my opinion. However, when I had to see how much he had destroyed your beautiful garden, I could no longer contain myself. I killed him in effect. He is buried under the turf. Please don't look, he looks terrible and I want to spare you this horrific sight. If you really love me, and you swore it to me, then don't tell anyone this deed. You will understand that I have to go abroad for a while. In deep love and affection
Your forever adoring darling.


Before he slipped the note under the front door, he opened the dog pen to allow the bulldog to lick the blood spatter in the garden. Then he swung himself into the beautiful limousine, put the key in the lock, stopped again briefly behind the garden hedge to collect the carrier bag with the other old dwarfs and took a deep breath. It was the nicest breath in a long time. His hand went into his pocket where he felt the diamonds, then he stroked the limo. Life had never been so good for him. He happily exclaimed “Live, I'm coming!” Then he accelerated and was never seen in this area again.
